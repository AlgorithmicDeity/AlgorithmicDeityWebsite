About Me

I’m Ashley, a passionate gamer, aspiring voice actor, and soon-to-be member of the modding community. My journey into voice acting, mocap, and pcap for video games is driven by a deep admiration for the kind of storytelling that leaves a lasting impact. Neil Newbon’s portrayal of Astarion is a perfect example—his work brought layers of emotion and complexity to a character in ways that challenged and moved the gaming community. This is the kind of difference I aspire to make, especially in how we address sensitive and important issues within our narratives. As I hone my coding skills, I’m also focused on building a safe and inclusive space for fellow gamers. My streams and videos are not just a place to share my love for games, but also a platform where I practice and refine my voice acting craft. My Discord community is open to all, embracing diversity and ensuring that everyone feels welcome—though we stand firm against any form of negativity or bullying. Join me weekly for epic RPGs, horror games, and life sims as we create, connect, and shape the worlds we love, one voice and one line of code at a time.
